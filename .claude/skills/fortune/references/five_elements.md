## Element Meanings

### Wood (木)
- **Personality**: Growth-oriented, compassionate, flexible, creative
- **Strengths**: Vision, generosity, cooperation
- **Weakness**: Indecisive, overly idealistic
- **Fortune**: Flourishes in spring; careers in education, arts, healing

### Fire (火)
- **Personality**: Passionate, dynamic, charismatic, restless
- **Strengths**: Leadership, enthusiasm, inspiration
- **Weakness**: Impulsive, aggressive under pressure
- **Fortune**: Peaks in summer; careers in entertainment, politics, sports

### Earth (土)
- **Personality**: Stable, nurturing, practical, loyal
- **Strengths**: Reliability, patience, common sense
- **Weakness**: Stubborn, overprotective
- **Fortune**: Steady throughout; careers in real estate, farming, medicine

### Metal (金)
- **Personality**: Disciplined, principled, strong-willed, organized
- **Strengths**: Determination, focus, justice
- **Weakness**: Rigid, inflexible, melancholic
- **Fortune**: Strongest in autumn; careers in finance, law, engineering

### Water (水)
- **Personality**: Wise, perceptive, adaptable, philosophical
- **Strengths**: Intuition, diplomacy, depth of thought
- **Weakness**: Over-cautious, fearful, withdrawn
- **Fortune**: Flows in winter; careers in writing, philosophy, research

## Elemental Interactions
- Wood feeds Fire → energizing partnership
- Fire creates Earth (ash) → transformative
- Earth bears Metal → productive
- Metal collects Water → supportive
- Water nourishes Wood → generative cycle
